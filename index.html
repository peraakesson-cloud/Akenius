Our way of working

Operator‑led consulting

02

Advisory

Commercial consultancy

Growth rarely stalls for lack of ideas. It stalls because strategy, processes and commercial execution stop reinforcing each other.

We take on focused engagements where the result is measurable:

  • Strategic planning and implementation. Most strategies fail in execution, not design. We help you make the real choices — and then make them happen in the organisation, with the discipline of someone who has had to live with the outcome.
  • Process and system mapping. Digital transformation built on unclear processes automates the confusion. We map how the work actually flows before deciding what technology should carry it.
  • Sales and marketing effectiveness. We accelerate what already works and fix what doesn't — pipeline, pricing conversations, channel focus, marketing that pulls its weight.
  • Pricing and profit optimization. Pricing is the fastest profit lever a company has, and the most neglected. We find the margin hiding in your price structure, discounting behaviour and product mix.

About

One principal, no bench.

Akenius AB was founded by Per Åkesson, formerly Director at VELUX with Nordic P&L responsibility, global pricing leadership and board experience including Kemp & Lauritzen A/S (Denmark). EMBA from Lund University; executive education at INSEAD.

The name carries a family history. Åkesson means son of Åke — and Åke was the founder's grandfather, an entrepreneur who ran his own businesses, as his father did before him. Akenius is the Latin form of that name, carried into a third generation of building companies.
