Smaller businesses and associations need a web presence that simply works — without an agency retainer, without chasing three different suppliers when something breaks.

We develop, host and care for websites and digital services as one accountable partner. Clear scope, clear pricing, no surprises. From build to deployment to keeping it running.

The problem we solve

Who do you call when it breaks?

For a small business or an association, the web usually looks like this: a designer built the site years ago, a hosting company owns the server, a third party set up the email — and when something breaks, each of them points at the other. Nobody is accountable for the whole, and the person chasing them is you.

The result is predictable: a site that slowly decays, invoices from three directions, and a sinking feeling every time something needs changing.

We replace that with one relationship. One partner who built it, runs it, and answers for it — with a fixed annual price and a scope you understood before you signed.

Where we deliver value

Build it, run it, keep it working

A new website — or a rescue of the one you have

We build modern, fast, mobile-first websites sized to what your organisation actually needs: presenting who you are, being found, and making it easy to contact, book or join. No overbuilt platform, no features you'll never use, no monthly surprise.

Where a site exists but has been abandoned by its makers, we take it over: move it to our care, fix what's broken, and give it an owner again.

Hosting and care

A website is not a project; it's a small piece of infrastructure. It needs hosting that stays up, software that stays updated, backups that actually exist, and someone who notices when something is wrong — before you do.

Our care agreement covers all of it: hosting, security updates, backups, monitoring, and a defined amount of content changes each year. One annual fee. One phone number.

Digital services around the site

The site is rarely the whole need. Custom email on your own domain, forms that reach the right person, simple booking or membership functions, integrations with the tools you already use. We handle the pieces that make the web presence a working part of the organisation — and we say so honestly when a need is beyond what we should build.
